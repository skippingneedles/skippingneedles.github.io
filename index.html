About Us

Julie Daniel

Co-Founder / Account Executive

One of my first music memories is sitting on the floor of my bedroom, ear glued to the radio, trying to catch my favorite song during a local station’s A-Z Beatles weekend. But I never heard it. Years later, I realized I’d been listening for Eggman instead of I Am the Walrus. No wonder I kept missing it!

My professional background includes 22 years in marketing, with an emphasis on account management, advertising, event management, prospecting, and social media outreach. With exceptional organization, attention to detail, and time management skills, I ensure everything runs smoothly so our clients can concentrate on what’s important; their music!

If you’re an up and coming musician in Portland and need help with the business side of this industry, message us. Let Skipping Needles help take your career to the next level!

Josh Kraeber

Co-Founder / Account Executive

My passion is music, especially live music. I’ve been going to shows across the U.S. for 15 years and have seen some of the best acts in the world. I want to share the knowledge I’ve picked up along the way and help shape the future of music.

My professional background includes 15 years in sales, with extensive experience advertising, promoting, and selling brands. I also understand the importance of listening to the client and know how to close the deal.

If you’re an up and coming musician in Portland and need help with the business side of this industry, message us. Let Skipping Needles partner with you and get you on track to living your dreams.
